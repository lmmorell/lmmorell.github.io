about

I am a passionate and detail-oriented computer science student, and a lover of any form of problem solving and design. I'm a believer that the key to maximizing success in emerging technologies comes down to not only practicality and efficiency, but elegance and simplicity as well.


Not only do I love solving problems in which I can apply my critical thinking skills, but I have a profound interest, and understand of, human psychology. I love being able to take advantage of opportunities that allow me to integrate my knowledge of psychology with computer science and technology; whether it be implementing user-focused design tactics, or picking apart machine learning algorithms.


Being able to apply my combined knowledge of different fields to solve problems is something that excites me for my professional journey and encourages me to learn as much as I possibly can from the resources I have at my fingertips.
